Suraj Kumar’s Top 3 Travel Destinations

B.Tech CSE (Honours), GLA University — Passionate Explorer & Tech Enthusiast

1. Gokarna, Karnataka

Gokarna is a peaceful beach town known for its scenic coastline, sacred temples, and relaxed vibe. A perfect weekend getaway from Mangalore to enjoy the sea breeze, sunsets, and serenity.

“Less crowded than Goa but equally beautiful — Gokarna is where calm meets adventure.”

2. Coorg (Madikeri), Karnataka

Popularly called the Scotland of India, Coorg offers lush coffee plantations, waterfalls, and misty hills. Ideal for nature walks, short treks, and refreshing air after a busy week of coding!

“Wake up to the aroma of coffee and the sound of waterfalls — Coorg is pure peace.”

3. Chikmagalur, Karnataka

Chikmagalur is a haven for trekkers and adventure lovers. Home to Karnataka’s highest peak, Mullayanagiri, it’s the perfect spot for a weekend filled with scenic views and a bit of adrenaline.

“Standing atop Mullayanagiri feels like touching the clouds — an unforgettable moment.”

Suraj’s Travel Tips

  • Plan weekend trips within 200 km to save time and money.
  • Carry a power bank and a compact travel kit — tech + travel ready!
  • Book stays early, especially during monsoon season in the Western Ghats.
  • Try local food — filter coffee in Coorg and seafood in Gokarna are must-haves.
  • Travel light, stay hydrated, and capture memories instead of things.
